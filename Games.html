24!


❮ ❯

Mobile Game - Casual
Made by Unity

This is a simple math game that makes people improve their skill and abilities at counting. You'll given 4 random numbers and try to operate it using 4 operators addition(+) , subtraction(-) , multiplication (x) , and division (÷) until it become 24! That's all, this game is really this simple as it looks. But some number combinations may be difficult to solve.

There are 2 mode in this game,

  • Casual mode, try to combine all the numbers to make 24, so you can go to the next level
  • Time limit, you have 45 second to combine all the numbers to make 24 and get points after solving it. You'll get higher points if you solve the problem faster. And be careful in this mode, the numbers not always can be solve! so you must click the "no-solution" button if it's impossible to solve.
